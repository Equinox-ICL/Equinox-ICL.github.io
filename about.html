E.quinox is a student-led humanitarian organisation at Imperial College London with the objective of uplifting rural Rwandan communities through research and technological innovation.

Comprising of students from diverse backgrounds and courses, we embrace our collaborative roots and combined skillset to create sustainable and affordable solutions to some of the poorest communities in the world.
